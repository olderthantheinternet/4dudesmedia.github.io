Storage Servers

Our Storage Server Array consists of 24 SuperMicro 4RU servers.
The 24 servers are connected to 2 load balancer. The specs for each of the 24 storage servers is as follows:

  • SuperMicro 4U 45 Bay 847E16-RJBOD1 3.5" SATA/SAS JBOD Chassis
  • 2x E5-2683 V4, 128GB RAM
  • AOC-S3008L-L8e SAS controller(s)
  • 100GB Network interface
  • 16 x 14TB Hard Disk Drives

In this configuration you are provided with redundant error free storage using CEPH technology

Custom Content Delivery Network

Global Content Delivery Network (CDN): Improves latency and performance with location-based access to our servers. By using OVH Nodes we can now performance at line speed across the fabric of their net. We are currently using nodes in key geographic locations - Currently we are providing nodes in the UK, DE, SG, and AU.

Our Backbone

Location on the West Coast of the USA. Our Global Internet Backbone provides IP Transit with low latency, access to thousands of networks, and dual-stack native IPv6+IPv4. The Backbone is connected to over 200 major exchange points and exchanges traffic directly with more than 7,500 different networks.
